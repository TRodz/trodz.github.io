RADISSON HOTEL

Summit Golf & Resort, Panama City

As a part of Summit Golf & Resort's master plan, a hotel was considered to fulfill social and sport activities in one of the most important golf clubs in Panama.
Radisson Hotel has 108 rooms adapted to the existant club house that was designed by the same firm in the year 2000.

Cost of the work: $7,000,000.00
